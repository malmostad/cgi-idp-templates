Cookies

This site requires two cookies to support authentication. A cookie is a small text file stored on your computer that stores information to be able to support automatic login to supported e-services during 60 minutes from successful eID authentication.

One of the cookies is a sessioncookie which contain a unique id and is removed automatically when you close your browser.

The other cookie contains information about which authentication server that managed your authentication and that cookie remains in your browser even after you restart the browser.

These cookies are needed to let you securely access different e-services as an authenticated user. The cookies contain no personal details and is not used by a third party

Avoid cookies

If you don't accept the use of cookies you can update the settings in your browser to deny cookies or be notified when a site wants to store a cookie. In the browser settings you can also delete previosly stored cookies. See the browsers help section for more information.

If you choose to not accept cookies you cannot use this authentication service.
